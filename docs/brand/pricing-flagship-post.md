# The strongest proof in this repo is not PRD writing

Most AI-for-PM content starts with PRDs.

I increasingly think that is not where the strongest proof lives.

The strongest proof lives in business judgment.

That is why the flagship case I care about most is not a PRD workflow.

It is this path:

`pricing-strategy -> commands/redesign-pricing-packages.md`

It does not answer:

“What price should we charge?”

It answers the harder questions:

- what is the real value metric
- where should the free-tier boundary sit
- what roles should membership, credits, export ability, and commercial rights each play
- what should trigger an upgrade
- which guardrails must be protected before rollout

When these decisions are right, monetization gets much easier.

When they are wrong, teams end up stuck in loops like:

- users do not understand what they are buying
- high-intent users are not captured cleanly
- free and paid layers fight each other
- every pricing discussion collapses back into “should we just raise prices”

I am not trying to build a pricing prompt.

I am trying to turn this kind of commercialization judgment into a reusable system that agents can execute correctly.

If you only inspect one proof point, start here:

- `docs/core/FLAGSHIP_CASES.md`
- `commands/redesign-pricing-packages.md`
- `commands/examples/redesign-pricing-packages-sample.md`
