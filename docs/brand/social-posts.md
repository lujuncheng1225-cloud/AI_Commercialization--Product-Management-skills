## Post 1: Point Of View

Most AI-for-PM content focuses on helping people write PRDs faster.

But I increasingly think the real value of AI for product managers is not writing faster. It is:

- asking the right questions
- forcing clearer decisions
- separating evidence from assumption
- making workflows reusable

That is why I built a `PM AI Skill Toolkit`.

It is not a prompt library.
It is a PM operating system for product managers and AI agents.

- Repo: [PM AI Skill Toolkit](https://github.com/lujuncheng1225-cloud/AI_Commercialization--Product-Management-skills)
- Demo: [Interactive Toolkit](https://lujuncheng1225-cloud.github.io/AI_Commercialization--Product-Management-skills/pm-skills-interactive-course.html)

## Post 3: Method

I increasingly think the most valuable thing to systematize for PMs in the AI era is not templates, but the decision chain:

`Clarify -> Decompose -> Decide -> Document -> Review`

If a system cannot help make that chain more reliable, it probably only helps people sound more polished, not work more professionally.

That is why I built a `PM AI Skill Toolkit` instead of a prompt library.
