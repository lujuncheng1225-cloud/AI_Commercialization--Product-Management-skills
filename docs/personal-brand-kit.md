# Personal Brand Kit

## Purpose

This document gives you a consistent way to describe yourself across platforms.

The goal is not to position you as "someone who knows AI tools."
The goal is to position you as:

- someone building AI products and commercialization systems
- someone focused on PM judgment and workflow design
- someone turning frontline product practice into reusable systems

## One-Line Positioning

### Minimal

Building AI products and turning PM practice into reusable systems.

### Standard

Product builder focused on AI products, commercialization, and reusable PM workflows for humans and agents.

### Strong-Point-Of-View

I care less about using AI to write faster, and more about using AI to help product teams make better, more reviewable decisions.

## What To Emphasize

Lead with these three ideas:

1. `AI products + commercialization`
2. `PM judgment + workflow systems`
3. `reusable systems for humans and agents`

Try not to lead with:

- prompts
- templates
- tool lists

Those are useful, but they are not what makes your work distinctive.

## Platform-Specific Recommendation

### For product communities

Focus on judgment, commercialization, and workflow systems.

### For technical communities

Focus on reusable agent-ready workflows and cross-tool PM systems.

### For founder / startup circles

Focus on AI products, commercialization leverage, and building operating systems instead of one-off hacks.
