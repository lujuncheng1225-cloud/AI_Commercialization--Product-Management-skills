- Product name: `[replace-me]`

## Target Users

- Primary users: individual contributors or team leads who need faster completion of recurring workflows
- Secondary users: managers who care about quality, throughput, and team adoption
- High-value segment: repeat users with strong task urgency and clear willingness to pay for better outcomes
- Anti-persona or non-target users: low-frequency casual users with weak urgency and low tolerance for onboarding friction

## Core User Problems

- Problem 1: users can reach initial value, but repeat usage and upgrade motivation are not yet strong enough
- Problem 2: AI capability sounds promising, but trust, quality bar, and fallback design are not always explicit
- Problem 3: commercial friction may exist between value moment and payment or upgrade moment

## Core Product Value

- Main value proposition: help users complete a meaningful workflow faster with acceptable quality and lower effort
- Key use case: accelerate a task that users already do repeatedly, rather than inventing a brand-new behavior
- What makes users come back: recurring need, saved time, clearer output, or higher-quality results

## Constraints

- Team constraints: PM and engineering bandwidth are limited; not every idea deserves a full PRD
- Tech constraints: model quality, latency, and fallback design all matter
- Compliance or policy constraints: do not assume sensitive data is automatically safe to process
- Time constraints: prefer staged rollout and experiment-first decisions when uncertainty is high

## Known Strategic Priorities

- Priority 1: improve repeatable user value before scaling noisy feature breadth
- Priority 2: tighten AI quality and evaluation before broad rollout
- Priority 3: improve monetization around high-intent moments instead of only adding more features

## Known Risks

- Risk 1: demo-quality AI gets mistaken for launch-quality AI
- Risk 2: commercial problems get oversimplified into pricing-only problems
- Risk 3: vague strategy language hides missing evidence and weak routing
