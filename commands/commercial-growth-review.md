# /commercial-growth-review

Review a commercial product problem with a growth and monetization lens.

## Workflow

1. Use `conversion-funnel-diagnostic` to identify the main drop-off.
2. Use `retention-levers-review` if the problem is downstream of activation.
3. Use `revenue-metrics-scorecard` to anchor the diagnosis in commercial metrics.
4. Use `growth-experiment-design` to propose the next tests.

## Checkpoints

- Separate acquisition problems from retention problems.
- Tie experiment ideas to one metric and one decision.
- Avoid changing pricing, onboarding, and messaging all at once.
