# Manifesto

## English

AI will not make great product managers obsolete.
It will make shallow product work easier to imitate.

That raises the bar for what good PMs must actually do:

- turn ambiguity into a clear problem
- separate evidence from assumption
- make trade-offs explicit
- choose simpler explanations before heavier ones
- design workflows, not just documents
- use AI as a force multiplier for judgment, not a substitute for it

This repository is built on a simple belief:

**great PM work in the AI era should be teachable, reusable, and operationalized.**

That means:

- not just faster output, but better structure
- not just more prompts, but better routing
- not just polished answers, but accountable decisions
- not just inspiration, but repeatable workflows

We do not want PMs to become prompt operators.
We want PMs to become stronger decision-makers with AI in the loop.

So this repo is intentionally opinionated.
It favors:

- clarification before generation
- decision before decoration
- evidence before confidence
- actionability before completeness
- systems over one-off hacks

If you are here to collect prompts, you may find this repo too structured.
If you are here to build a stronger PM practice for the AI era, welcome.

## 中文

AI 不会让优秀产品经理失去价值。
它更可能先让浅层、模板化的产品工作变得更容易被复制。

这反而抬高了好产品经理真正要做的事：

- 把模糊需求收敛成清晰问题
- 区分证据、推断和假设
- 明确 trade-off，而不是堆建议
- 在复杂解释之前先检验更简单的解释
- 设计工作流，而不只是生成文档
- 把 AI 当作判断力的放大器，而不是替代品

这个仓库建立在一个很朴素的信念上：

**AI 时代优秀的 PM 工作，应该可以被教学、复用，并最终被系统化。**

所以这套库想做的不是：

- 只是更快地产出内容
- 只是收集更多 Prompt
- 只是生成看起来专业的答案

而是：

- 让路由更稳定
- 让判断更明确
- 让产出更可复查
- 让工作流可以被人和 agent 共同复用

这也是为什么这个仓库会刻意强调：

- 先澄清，再生成
- 先判断，再修饰
- 先证据，再置信度
- 先可执行，再求完整
- 优先系统，而不是一次性技巧

如果你只是来找几个 Prompt，这个仓库可能显得有点“重”。
但如果你想构建一套 AI 时代真正能长期复用的产品工作方式，这里就是为你准备的。
